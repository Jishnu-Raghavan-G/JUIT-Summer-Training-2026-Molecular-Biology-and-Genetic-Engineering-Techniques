# JUIT Summer Training Programme

## Molecular Biology and Genetic Engineering Techniques

> **Personal Laboratory Notebook & Learning Portfolio**

---

## Overview

This repository documents my learning, practical experience, and scientific observations during the **Summer Training Programme on Molecular Biology and Genetic Engineering Techniques** conducted at **Jaypee University of Information Technology (JUIT), Solan**.

It has been developed as a structured laboratory notebook that combines theoretical concepts, experimental workflows, laboratory techniques, practical observations, and personal reflections acquired throughout the training.

Rather than serving as a conventional laboratory record, this repository focuses on understanding the scientific principles behind each experiment, documenting laboratory practices, and organizing knowledge into a long-term reference for future research and advanced studies.
